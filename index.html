Getting Started - Welcome to Namu6!

You are currently viewing the homepage. This page acts as an introduction or start point for the rest of your website.

 

When you have finished reading this introduction, you can replace it with your own content.

 

 

Choosing a theme: Click the theme picture at the top of the page, then click the 'Theme Menu' button then click "Choose Theme'

 

Add a picture: Click here then click the 'Text Menu' button then 'Picture'

 

Remove a picture: Click on the picture then click the 'Picture Menu' button then click 'Delete Picture'.

 

Create a link: Highlight the text that you want to be linked then click the 'Text Menu' button, then choose the type of link you wish to create.

 

You can follow links by clicking on them. Click the 'Enable Links' button at the top of the page to enable links.

 

Navigation links a top of page: You can add links in the navigation box the same way text links are created.

 

 

Place content in this side column by clicking here and typing.
